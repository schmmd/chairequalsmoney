Gameplay

The rules to Chair Equals Money are similar to those of Sokoban. The man can walk either North, West, South, or East. He is controlled by the four arrow keys. In addition to walking, he can also push a single chair , but he may neither pull a chair nor push two chairs at once. If a chair is pushed onto a base , it turns to money . If money is pushed off of a base , it reverts to a chair . The objective is to turn all of the chairs into money .


Beware: the simple rules only betray the complex strategy required!
